In this week you will map an EER Diagram to logical (relational) diagram using SQL Server Management Studio (Database Diagram section under the database you created).

a) Use nice names for Columns, Keys, Constraints and Views.  
b) Use proper domains for the fields that provides a good data representation and compact storage.  
c) Decide field as 'Nullable' or 'Not null' wisely. 
d) Determine best 'Primary key' for each table.
e) Determine proper 'Foreign keys'.
